All app features

- Campaigns & sessions : create campaigns, store sheets / NPCs / orgs / Night City maps / documents per campaign on the GM's machine. Open a campaign into a unified, browser-style tabbed workspace.

- Go live & cast : a Go-live toggle turns the workspace into a live session. The GM casts reveals — text, images, NPC dossiers, locations — to every player as a Silver-Case "Film Window" overlay, with a control monitor showing what's on air.

- Party & live sheets : every connected player's sheet, embedded and editable live by the GM, in columns, tabs or a grid. Players edit their own; changes sync both ways instantly.

- Map : the full Night City district map plus custom maps and off-map location lists, with GM/player layers, public/private places, and a redesigned location popup (image gallery, view/edit, open-shop).

- Combat : the real CP2020 combat engine — initiative, damage, wounds, ammo, status — launched with the party plus generated NPCs or saved squads; turns can be cast to players.

- NPC & ORG : a bestiary of campaign NPCs (quick generator or the full NPC sheet), an organisation generator and the full Organisations tool, each saveable and revealable.

- Shop : the GM curates Database items (drag & drop) into storefronts linked to locations, NPCs or orgs, with prices, stock and sales pitches. Players browse a card storefront, compare gear against what they own, and buy — purchases land on the sheet and in the buy tray; a default online catalogue is always available.

- Generators : roll combat-ready NPCs, whole squads, and nested loot containers (money, IP, items) that can be granted straight onto player sheets.

- Clocks : tension trackers in several visual styles, advanced and cast to players.

- Database : every reference list from the site (weapons, cyberware, gear, vehicles, decks, programs, skills, roles, corporations) plus your saved NPCs, orgs and custom entries — searchable, with a custom-entry form.

- Log & Sourcebooks : a timestamped session journal of casts, combat and loot, and the "Établi" sourcebook reader for your PDFs.

- Local-first : everything runs on the GM's machine over the LAN — no accounts, no cloud, no fees. Sheets persist as plain JSON files in a visible campaign folder.
